Travel

Arriving on Taniti

✈️ By Air

Taniti is served by a small airport that accommodates small jets and propeller planes. The airport is currently being expanded to handle larger jets in the future.

🚢 By Sea

Taniti's port at Yellow Leaf Bay welcomes cruise ships and private boats. The port is a popular entry point for visitors arriving by sea.

Getting Around

🚌 Public Bus

Taniti City is served by a reliable public bus system that runs frequently throughout the day.

🚐 Private Bus

Private bus services are available for hire and can take you anywhere on the island.

🚕 Taxi

Taxis are available throughout Taniti City and surrounding areas. Most drivers speak some English.

🚗 Rental Car

Rental cars are available, though roads outside of Taniti City may be unpaved.

🚲 Bike Rental

Bicycles are available for rent and are a popular way to explore the island at your own pace.

🚶 Walking

Many attractions in Taniti City and Merriton Landing are within walking distance of each other.

Good to Know

🏥 Medical

Taniti has one hospital with multilingual staff and several clinics for minor medical needs.

🗓️ Holidays

Many businesses close on national holidays. Please plan your travel and activities accordingly.

🔒 Safety

Taniti is a safe destination. Violent crime is rare, though visitors should watch for pickpocketing in tourist areas.
