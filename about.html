About Taniti

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific. Our population of about 20,000 occupies just under 500 square miles of lush paradise. We welcome you to come and enjoy our one of kind vacation and experience destination.

Good to Know:

  • Currency: Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its primary currency, though euros and yen are also widely accepted.
  • Language: English is the primary language spoken by most younger locals.
  • Medical: Taniti is home to a hospital with multilingual staff as well as several small clinics.
  • Power: Just like the U.S. Taniti uses 120v outlets.
  • Drinking Age: Taniti's legal drinking age is 18.
  • Alcohol hours:Alcohol is not served between midnight to 9:00 A.M..
  • Safety: While violent crime is rare, watch for pickpocketing in tourist areas.
  • Holidays: Taniti celebrates many closures on national holidays, please plan ahead.

Notable locations:

Taniti City:

Main hub, native architecture, beaches

Yellow Leaf Bay:

Harbor, Cruise ship dock

Merriton Landing:

North side, Rapidly developing, lots of activities

Medical Access:

Hospital:

Taniti has one hospital with multilingual staff available to assist international visitors.

Clinics:

Several clinics are located on the island for minor medical needs.

Travel Insurance:

Visitors are encouraged to obtain travel health insurance before arriving.
